Our Commitment to Your Privacy

How we protect your information for cab bookings and car rentals.

  • 1.

    Data Collection: We collect customer details such as name, phone number, email address, and travel requirements only for booking and service purposes.

  • 2.

    Data Usage: Personal information will be used strictly for reservations, billing, and customer support.

  • 3.

    Confidentiality: We do not share or sell customer information to third parties, except where required by law.

  • 4.

    Payments: Any payment-related information shared with us is handled securely. We do not store sensitive banking details.

  • 5.

    Communication: By booking with us, you agree to receive booking confirmations, service updates, and essential communication through SMS, email, or phone.

  • 6.

    Customer Rights: Customers can request corrections or deletion of their personal data by contacting us directly.

  • 7.

    Policy Updates: This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be notified on our official communication channels.
